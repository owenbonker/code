Frequently Ask Question

How can I schedule an appointment?

Scheduling an appointment at Newo Dental Clinic is easy! You can call us directly at (+63)999 563 3412, or you can book online through our website. If you prefer, you can also email us at Newodental@gmail.com with your preferred date and time, and our staff will get back to you promptly to confirm your appointment.

How should I prepare for my first visit?

To prepare for your first visit to Newo Dental Clinic, please arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork. Bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and a list of any medications you are currently taking. If you have previous dental records or X-rays, please bring those as well. It's also helpful to have a list of any questions or concerns you might want to discuss with your dentist.
